Mukhtar Shakhanov

Seventh sense

So you got married.
I’m happy for you.
But it’s not only people –
wolves and she-wolves
join their fates,
in order
to avoid loneliness
and provide
lifelong support
to one another.
To marry like everyone does,
and produce a child,
doesn’t require
great intelligence.
The question in all of this,
is how will the face that
now looks you in the mirror
be judged tomorrow?
