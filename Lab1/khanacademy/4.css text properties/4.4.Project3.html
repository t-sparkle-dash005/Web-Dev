Book recomendation. The Long Walk by Stephen King

18.01.2025

Each year one hundred boys under the age of eighteen enter into a walking competition where there can only be one winner. Boys around the country enter to compete despite knowing there will be only one survivor. The reward is too high not to be drawn in. The idea that you can have whatever you want for the rest of your life is something they are willing to risk everything for a chance at having. Among this year’s contestants is sixteen-year-old Ray Garraty and he knows just what to do: keep a pace of four miles per hour without stopping for anything or anyone. Three warnings and you’re out – permanently. Reasons why you should read this:

  1. It's easy and fast to read
  2. The book shows how people's behaviour change under different conditions
  3. Author perfectly describes atmosphere of competition
