On January 10, 2025 at 16.00 in the State Museum of Arts of the RK named after Abylkhan Kasteyev, will be the opening of the International Exhibition-Symposium "TURAN TAULARY" dedicated to the 120th anniversary of the birth of Abylkhan Kasteyev and Oral Tansykbayev, as well as the presentation of the catalog will take place.

Where:Koktem-3 microdistrict, 22/1, Almaty
