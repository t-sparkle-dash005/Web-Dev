Can you solve these problems?

1. Which stones cannot be found in a river?

2. What cannot fit even into the largest pot?

3. What can be standing and going, standing
and hanging on, going and lying down at the same time?

4. The farm had two horses, one rabbit, one
puppy, one cat, a pig, and a piglet, a cow and a calf, a turkey, and a
goose. The owner came there with the dog. How many feet are there on the farm?

5. Three tortoises are crawling. The first
tortoise says: two tortoises are crawling after me. The second tortoise
says: one tortoise crawls after me and another one in front of me. And
the third tortoise says: two tortoises are crawling in front of me, and
another one behind me. How can this be?
